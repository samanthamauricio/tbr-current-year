My TBR of 2025

Last year I felt overwhelmed by all the new authors, must-reads, tier lists and tropes that suddenly appeared in the book community as a result of Book Tok, so I decided to read all of the books that won the Goodreads Choice Award in the last few years. And that... obviously and unfortunately... did not happen.

That being said, even if I did not succeed in reading 45 books, I can proudly say that there wasn't a single month in the year in which I wasn't reading at least one book. And that's more than a lot of people can say.

The genres that I enjoy the most are Romance, Romantasy and Fantasy, so those were the categories that I wanted to read the most. I was aiming to read not just the winner of the Goodreads choice awards, but also the second and third place for those categories, in case the winner was part of a series. Ultimately, the books I added to my TBR were the following:

Romance:

  1. 2024: Funny Story by Emily Henry.
  2. 2023: Happy Place by Emily Henry.
  3. 2022: Book Lovers by Emily Henry.
  4. 2021: People We Meet on Vacation by Emily Henry.
  5. 2020: From Blood and Ash by Jennifer L. Armentrout.

Fantasy:

  1. 2024: Somewhere Beyond the Sea by T.J. Klune.
  2. 2023: Hell Bent by Leigh Bardugo.
  3. 2022: House of Sky and Breath by Sarah J. Maas.

Romantasy:

  1. 2024: House of Flame and Shadow by Sarah J. Maas.
  2. 2023: Fourth Wing by Rebecca Yarros
  3. 2023: Assistant to the Villain by Hannah Nicole Maehrer.
  4. 2023: The Ashes & the Star-Cursed King by Carissa Broadbent.
  5. 2023: A Soul of Ash and Blood by Jennifer L. Armentrout.
