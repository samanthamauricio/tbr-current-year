Some of the books I read this year:

  • The Invisible life of Addie LaRue

Author: V.E. Shwab.

Genre: Fantasy.

Summary: In 1714, France, a young girl makes a deal with the devil to live forever, and he grants it under the condition that she will be immediately forgotten as soon as she is out of sight. She lives like that for 300 years until she meets a boy who, for some reason, remembers her.

Rating: 5/5.

  • The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

Author: Taylor Jenkins-Reid.

Genre: Historical Fiction.

Summary: Monique, a regular journalist is suddenly told that the world-famous Oscar-winning actress Evelyn Hugo wants to do an autobiography and wants her to be the author. For the first time ever, Evelyn Hugo tells the story of her seven husbands and how she became the legend the world knew, which is somehow related to Monique much more than she would've imagined.

Rating: 5/5.

  • Funny Story

Author: Emily Henry.

Genre: Contemporary Romance.

Summary: After a long relationship and engagement, Daphne is dumped a few weeks before the wedding. Without a place to stay, she moves in with the ex-boyfriend of the girl her boyfriend cheated on her with!

Rating: 4.5/5.

  • The Accidental Dating Experiment

Author: Lauren Blakely.

Genre: Contemporary romance.

Summary: Two friends, who have a podcast together, are given a house by a listener, and go to a small town to check it out. Since the protagonist has been unlucky in the dating department, the co-star decides to pretend to be a first-date to help her find red flags and they eventually fall in love.

Rating: 2.5/5.

  • Play Along

Author: Liz Tomforde.

Genre: New adult.

Summary: After working as an athetic trainer for years, and keeping all the attractive baseball players at an arms-lenght, Kennedy gets drunk in Vegas and ends up marrying the team capitan

Rating: 4/5.

  • King of Wrath

Author: Ana Huang.

Genre: New adult.

Summary:Billionare Dante Russo is blackmailed to marry a jewelry heiress. While he agrees, both of them do little to hide their disdain for one another... until the hatred turns into something else.

Rating: 4/5.

  • Book lovers

Author: Emily Henry.

Genre: Contemporary romance.

Summary:A literary agent and a book editor, who have hated each other for years, end up working on the same project... and discover that they have much more in common than the would've imagined.

Rating: 5/5.

  • A Witch's Guide to Fake Dating a Demon

Author: Sarah Hawley.

Genre: Romantasy.

Summary: A witch mistakenly summons a demon while trying to bake a cake, and he refuses to leave without taking her soul with him.

Rating: 1/5.

  • Twisted Lies

Author: Ana Huang.

Genre: New adult.

Summary: The CEO of a security company falls in love with an influencer who has been stalked for years by a misterious follower who is obsessed with her.

Rating: 2.5/5.

  • Ready Player One

Author: Ernest Cline.

Genre: Science Ficton.

Summary: In a world where the virtual reality has become the real world for most of the population, the creator of Oasis (the game the whole world is governed by) passes away and offers his fortune to the person capable of finding the hidden treasure in his game.

Rating: 5/5.

  • Loathe to love you

Author: Ali Hazelwood.

Genre: Contemporary romance.

Summary: Three short stories of enemies-to-lovers.

Rating: 3/5.
